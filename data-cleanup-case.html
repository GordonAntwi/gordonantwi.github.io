Data Verification & Cleanup for High-Value Outreach

The Challenge: Dirty Data & Domain Risk

The provided database was a mixture of old records and unverified leads. Launching a campaign with this list would not only waste time but could seriously harm the client's sender reputation by generating an unacceptably high bounce rate.

The client required absolute assurance that the final list was clean, enriched with relevant job titles, and ready for immediate deployment without causing domain damage.

Figure 1.1: The Final Enriched Dataset. A screenshot of the master spreadsheet, showing the clean, filtered output with the confirmed Title and technically Verified Email columns ready for immediate campaign launch.

My Solution: Verification & Enrichment Pipeline

1.

Initial Data Scrub: Used Google Sheets and custom formulas to eliminate duplicates, re-format inconsistencies, and normalize all names and company names.

2.

Email Verification (The 0% Bounce Assurance): Utilized a professional email verification tool to check every single address, categorizing them into deliverable, risky, and undeliverable. Only deliverable contacts were kept.

3.

Data Enrichment: Manually and semi-automatically researched the remaining contacts to confirm their current Job Title and LinkedIn Profile to ensure the cold outreach was highly personalized and targeted.

Figure 2.1: The Verification Assurance Report. A dashboard view of the final list quality, clearly displaying a 0% Bounce Rate assurance, which protects the client's sender reputation and guarantees a successful campaign.
