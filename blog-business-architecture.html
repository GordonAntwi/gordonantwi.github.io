The Pyramid Insight (This Changes How You See Growth)

A pyramid is the only structure where more doesn't mean weaker.

Why?

Because in a pyramid:

  • Height is a secondary effect of width
  • Growth is proportional
  • The apex rises only when the base expands

You don't "reach" the top. You earn the top.

This is the core idea behind Business Architecture for Tomorrow.
