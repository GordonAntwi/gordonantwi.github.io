Case Study 01

Restoring Email Deliverability & Automating M365 Sales Triage

A B2B marketing firm was losing over 75% of cold outreach leads due to a critical domain reputation collapse. My role was to diagnose, secure, and automate their lead workflow.

The Challenge: Domain Collapse

The client's domain was flagged across major email providers due to misconfigured DNS records (SPF, DKIM, and DMARC). This was causing a massive 78% email bounce rate, meaning valuable sales leads were never even seeing their pitch.

Furthermore, the few leads that did convert were being manually triaged from a shared inbox into the CRM by the sales team, a repetitive task wasting roughly 4 hours per person each week.

Figure 1.1: BEFORE: Initial Deliverability Failure. DNS analytics tool, clearly showing the red-alert status of a 78% email failure rate due to critical DMARC and SPF errors before my intervention.

My Solution: Technical Repair & Automation

1.

Deliverability Restoration: I conducted a full DNS audit and implemented correct DMARC, SPF, and DKIM records within 48 hours. I also created a monitoring dashboard to ensure ongoing compliance, instantly boosting deliverability.

2.

Automated Triage System: I built an M365 Power Automate flow that monitors the sales inbox for specific keywords (e.g., "demo request," "pricing"). This flow automatically creates a new, prioritized entry in the CRM and assigns it to the next available sales representative.

3.

Team Training: I documented the new process in a concise Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) and trained the sales team to ensure smooth adoption, maximizing the new system‚Äôs efficiency gains.
